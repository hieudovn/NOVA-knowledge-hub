# Condition Monitoring

## Summary

Condition monitoring capability tracks asset or process condition so teams can detect abnormal behavior and act before failure or loss.

## Common Needs

- Condition indicators and alarm thresholds.
- Trend analysis and diagnostics.
- Asset-specific monitoring models.
- Integration with maintenance workflows.
