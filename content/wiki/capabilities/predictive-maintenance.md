# Predictive Maintenance

## Summary

Predictive maintenance capability uses data, rules, analytics, or models to anticipate failures and recommend maintenance actions.

## Common Needs

- Failure pattern detection.
- Remaining useful life or risk indication.
- Maintenance recommendation support.
- Feedback loop from work execution and asset history.
