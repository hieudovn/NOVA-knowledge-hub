# Industrial Historian

## Summary

Industrial historian capability covers collection, compression, storage, retrieval, and visualization of time-series operations data.

## Common Needs

- Reliable time-series storage.
- Fast retrieval for operations and engineering users.
- Integration with SCADA, DCS, PLC, and analytics systems.
- Retention, redundancy, and data governance.
