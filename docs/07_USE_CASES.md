# Use Cases

## 1. Identify Customers To Approach

Question:

Which industrial customers should Avenue approach next?

Required data:

- Customer records;
- industry and segment;
- plant/facility records;
- market signals;
- relationship history;
- Avenue solution fit;
- current or likely procurement activity.

Output:

- ranked customer or plant list;
- supporting evidence;
- suggested entry strategy;
- owner and next action.

## 2. Detect Plant-Level Opportunity

Question:

Which plants or facilities are likely to need Avenue solutions?

Required data:

- plant profile;
- production process;
- known systems;
- reliability, maintenance, ESG, expansion, or digital transformation signals;
- relevant Avenue capabilities;
- case studies or references.

Output:

- Opportunity candidate;
- confidence score;
- assumptions;
- suggested technical angle.

## 3. Map Tender To Avenue Capability

Question:

Can Avenue respond to this tender, and what strategy should be used?

Required data:

- tender scope;
- technical requirements;
- commercial requirements;
- compliance requirements;
- timeline;
- competitors;
- Avenue solutions, partners, references, and risks.

Output:

- compliance matrix candidate;
- solution fit;
- bid/no-bid recommendation input;
- tender strategy;
- presales action plan.

## 4. Turn Market Signal Into Opportunity Candidate

Question:

Does this public announcement, investment project, recruitment signal, or regulation change indicate a potential opportunity?

Required data:

- signal record;
- source URL;
- source reliability;
- customer or plant match;
- industry;
- likely pain point or trigger;
- relevant Avenue offerings.

Output:

- unverified or reviewed Opportunity candidate;
- evidence summary;
- assumptions to validate;
- research tasks.

## 5. Recommend Sales And Presales Action

Question:

What should Sales, Presales, Technical, and Management do next?

Required data:

- Opportunity record;
- customer and plant context;
- tender/procurement status;
- contact and stakeholder map;
- relationship history;
- solution fit;
- risks and constraints.

Output:

- role-based action plan;
- message angle;
- required preparation;
- meeting or outreach objective;
- review status.

## 6. Protect Knowledge Quality

Question:

Is this Opportunity supported well enough to act on?

Required data:

- evidence items;
- assumptions;
- source timestamps;
- confidence score;
- conflicting sources;
- human review state.

Output:

- approval, rejection, or research request;
- governance findings;
- audit trail.
